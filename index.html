Interactive Earth Engine Analysis

Satellite imagery has emerged as a pivotal resource for the remote monitoring of mangroves on a global scale. Google Earth Engine (GEE) offers access to an extensive repository of satellite imagery. Leveraging Landsat 8/9 satellite data, we employ the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) to yield vital insights into mangrove health and ecosystem vitality.
